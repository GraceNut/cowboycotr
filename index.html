What is Cowboy Church on the Rock?

Cowboy Church on the Rock was established to fill a need in our community for honest, straightforward worship and Bible teaching. No matter whether you are a cowboy or cowgirl, businessman or woman, mom or pop, small child or teenager you will find that warm special relationship you are seeking.

Cowboy Church on the Rock is here to help meet your needs in this fast-growing, untamed world by offering you a way of enjoying your friends, family and animals.

So come on out and let's have some fun!
