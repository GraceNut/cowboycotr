Events

Christian Based Fellowship

We know how families are looking to enjoy their animals and get out of the house so we encourage good clean fun and fellowship (muddy boots are acceptable) that is non-offensive to others around you including families with young children. We are convinced that there is plenty of fun to be had.

Free Trail Rides

Cowboy Church on the Rock continues to host free family friendly trail rides all over southeast texas. Including - Ebenezer Park, Crystal Beach, Cattail Marsh at Tyrrell Park. Please call Pastor Kenny's cell phone (409) 289-2400 or email.

Young Wranglers Events

The Young'ins can enjoy some fun while you participate in the event. They can even ride for FREE! We have volunteers who allow others to ride their fine horses. These Wranglers will lead first timers around the arena on their horses and instruct more experienced riders on their horsemanship. Other fun-filled activities may include: chicken chases, roping contests, sack races, prizes and more! In the future, horsemanship lessons and other events will be offered.

Tyrrell Park

The Tyrrell Park facilities include over 12 miles of equestrian trails as well as a variety of other activities such as golf and bird watching in man-made wetlands. Guided tours on horseback are available by appointment.

Equestrian Events

Obstacle Course

The obstacle course may include weaving through poles, racing around barrels, and crossing over a bridge. A two second penalty is incurred for each missed obstacle.

Ranch Sorting, and Open Arena Sorting

During these events each cow is numbered and the time starts when the announcer calls out the first number to be cut out of the herd. If a cow crosses into the capture area out of order the attempt is over and no time is scored.
